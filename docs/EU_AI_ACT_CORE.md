# EU AI Act Reference for Compliance Agents (Status: March 2026)

## 1. Prohibited AI Practices (Article 5)
*ANY system matching these is classified as UNACCEPTABLE RISK and is BANNED.*
- **Subliminal Techniques:** Systems that use subliminal components beyond a person's consciousness to distort behavior.
- **Vulnerability Exploitation:** Exploiting specific groups based on age, disability, or socio-economic situation.
- **Social Scoring:** Evaluation or classification of people based on social behavior or personality traits by public or private actors.
- **Predictive Policing:** Assessing the risk of a person committing a crime based solely on profiling.
- **Biometric Scraping:** Untargeted scraping of facial images from the internet or CCTV.

## 2. High-Risk AI Systems (Article 6 & Annex III)
*Systems in these categories require a Conformity Assessment and CE Marking.*
- **Biometrics:** Remote biometric identification, emotion recognition, or biometric categorization.
- **Critical Infrastructure:** Safety components in water, gas, heating, and electricity management.
- **Education:** Systems determining access to institutions or assessing student performance.
- **Employment/HR:** Recruitment tools, task allocation based on traits, or performance monitoring.
- **Essential Services:** Credit scoring, insurance pricing, or emergency service triage (e.g., 112).
- **Law Enforcement:** Evidence reliability evaluation or profiling individuals.
- **Migration/Border Control:** Automated visa examination or security assessments.

## 3. High-Risk Exception (Article 6.3)
An Annex III system is NOT high-risk if:
- It performs a narrow procedural task.
- It improves the result of a previously completed human activity.
- It is only intended to detect decision-making patterns without influencing human assessment.
- *Note: If the system performs profiling of natural persons, it is ALWAYS high-risk.*

## 4. Transparency Obligations (Article 50)
*Applies to non-high-risk systems like chatbots and generative AI.*
- **Interaction Disclosure:** Users must be informed they are interacting with an AI.
- **Content Marking:** AI-generated text/images must be marked in a machine-readable format.
- **Deepfakes:** Must be clearly labeled as manipulated content.

## 5. General Purpose AI (GPAI) Models (Article 51-55)
- **Standard GPAI:** Must provide technical documentation and instructions for use.
- **Systemic Risk GPAI:** (Trained with >10^25 FLOPS). Requires adversarial testing and systemic risk mitigation.
